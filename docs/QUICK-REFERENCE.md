## Tips for New Users

### The Menu Button

The **Menu button** (bottom-left corner) is your starting point for everything. Click it and start typing to search for any application or setting.

### Installing Software

Unlike macOS or Windows, you don't download apps from websites. Instead:

1. Open **Software Manager** from the menu
2. Search for what you want
3. Click **Install**
4. Enter your password
5. Done!

### File Locations

- **Home folder:** All your personal files
- **Documents, Downloads, Pictures, etc.:** Inside your home folder
- **USB drives:** Appear on the desktop and in Files sidebar
